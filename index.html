A well designed buisness is a beautiful thing...

Good design can take many shapes- eye catching graphics, a skillfully crafted marketing plan, a creative web presense. These things are never more beautiful to me as when a business first come to life or during periods of metamophiss. That is why I specialize early stage, small businesses or those going through a period of transition. Good design never happens accidentally, so if you are looking to establish a consistant look and feel in your young buisness or are trying to revitalize or reband; you need to know how all of the pieces are going to fit together. I see the big picture and have small business experience to back it up. You can be small and still think big and I can help you do that.
